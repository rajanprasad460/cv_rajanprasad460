Research Summary

I am an interdisciplinary researcher working at the intersection of intelligent autonomous systems, robotics, biomechanics, and human-centered AI. My research focuses on developing adaptive robotic systems capable of interacting with complex environments and human users through advanced control, sensing, and learning frameworks.

  • Autonomous robotic systems, including UGVs and manipulators
  • Bio-inspired and soft robotics, including continuum manipulators
  • Wearable robotics and rehabilitation systems
  • Biosignal-driven human-in-the-loop control systems

Long-term goal: To establish a research program on intelligent autonomous systems integrating AI, multimodal sensing, dynamics & control, and human-robot collaboration.
